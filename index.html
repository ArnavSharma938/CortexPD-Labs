GeneLang

Crafting sequences, one nucleotide at a time.

GeneLang is designed to revolutionize the field of genomics by providing an intuitive and powerful platform for analyzing, manipulating, and understanding DNA and RNA sequences. Created by Arnav Sharma, the language aims to empower researchers and bioinformaticians with tools that streamline genetic sequence operations, promote deeper insights, and drive innovation in genetic research.
